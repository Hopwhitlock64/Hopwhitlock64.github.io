Overview

Purpose

To provide efficient and accurate weather information such as temperature, wind speed and percipitation. By doing so people can more prepared for the days and weeks ahead.

Audience

Weather affects everyone and it can be very useful and sometimes even life saving. Whether its just a regular sunny day, a snow storm or hurricane, its good to be ready for it. The global weather channel aims to povide accurate and efficient global weather to anyone wanting to know the daily, weekly or monthly weather for their current location. The information provided will be the same no matter who or where you are
