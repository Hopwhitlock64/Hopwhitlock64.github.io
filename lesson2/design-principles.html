PaRC: Alignment

V-Moda

V-Moda

Their top navigation bar is horizontally and vertically aligned in the center. All the images are centrtalized and the same size. The paragraph and head lines is centerd. The content makes it very clear to the audience on what they do and sell.

PARC: Contrast

Spotify

Spotify

Spotify shows this element through their color scheme but also through the sizing of their button.
The main is in their background image and their button.
They also show contrast at the bottom with white letters against a black background.

Visual Hierarchy

Amazon

Amazon

Amazon shows this design element through its layout of the nav bar, images and buttons. Its also shown with their item placements. The Logo and search bar is right at the top along with the shopping cart and menu icon. This is visual hierarchy because they put the most important items at the top then other product placements as you go down.
